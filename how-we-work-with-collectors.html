How we work with Collectors

CFASS works with established and emerging collectors of art, antiques, and collectibles. Our Singapore FreePort facility can also accommodate collections of both wine and jewelry with its dedicated wine vaults and jewelry safety deposit boxes.

Collectors come to CFASS when their current home viewing or storage space is inadequate and to enjoy the peace of mind that their property is safe and preserved for generations to come.

We start our relationship with collectors by initiating a dialogue about specific requirements. From there, we recommend an appropriate storage and service solution. CFASS is pleased to assist with handling a single item to a vast collection, and for a period of days to many years. While property is at CFASS, collectors can take advantage of our broad service offerings, including collection management, appraisals, insurance, escrow agent services, loan collateral management, and private sales.
