Foundations

CFASS can serve as the single point of contact for all artist foundations' art service needs. We are built to store and preserve art for decades, and our concierge services and simplified custom pricing structures particularly appeal to these types of institutions.

Foundations also come to CFASS when they are considering a long-term deaccesion plan. We assist with every step of this process, from assessing the foundation's service requirements to developing a long-term plan with pricing that allows for a predictable cost outlay. We also arrange for all requested services, including packing, shipping, consolidating, storing, appraising, and insuring property.

We are pleased to offer foundations private tours of our facility space to provide a clear understanding of our unique business.
