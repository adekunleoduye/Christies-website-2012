Wealth Advisors and Lenders

CFASS offers smart solutions for the management of art assets. We are pleased to serve as the single point of contact for wealth advisors' and lenders' art service needs. Our complete neutrality, fail-safe facilities, and concierge services particularly appeal to professionals and clients of wealth advisory groups and those providing liquidity and/or lines of credit.

Wealth advisors and lenders come to CFASS to support their clients' collection management and liquidity needs. We help clients smartly manage their art assets by storing property in our state-of-the-art facilities. From there we assist with services such as appraisals and insurance to ensure the collection has appropriate coverage. For lenders providing liquidity for full-recourse or non-recourse loans, we offer loan collateral management. We manage collateral used against a loan in accordance with the terms set forth in a client and lending institution's contractual agreement.

We are pleased to offer wealth advisors and lenders private tours of our facility space to provide a clear understanding of our unique business.
