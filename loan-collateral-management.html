Loan Collateral Management

CFASS works on the client's behalf to reach out to lending institutions and negotiate loans against the property stored in our facilities. We introduce lenders who will loan the funds and create an appropriate financial vehicle to meet the client's unique needs. CFASS then manages the collateral in our state-of-the-art facility in accordance with the terms set forth in the client and lending institution's contractual agreement.

To learn more about this and other services,
feel free to contact us.
