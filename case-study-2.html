Case Study 2: A Multi-Family Office

A multi-family office came to CFASS to assist with the management of their family's collection of valuable paintings and antique furniture. The family, with their Trustee, decided to appraise and de-accession a significant portion of the collection, and CFASS was recommended to manage the project.

CFASS worked closely with the Trustee and family office team, advised them throughout the process, and implemented solutions to reach the family's desired outcome. After the collection was safely received at CFASS, the CFASS team unpacked, photographed, and inspected each item. All details and images were uploaded onto the family's private MyCFASS online account, and the collection was installed into a viewing gallery. Various experts from auction houses, museums, foundations, and galleries viewed the collection and gave their opinions on value and de-accession options. CFASS then advised the family office on the best course of action, communicated and negotiated on the family's behalf with interested parties, and prepared the collection for release. The family office enjoyed CFASS as a single trusted contact, realized significant advantages in negotiations with interested parties, accomplished the goal of successfully de-accessioning the collection, and protected its clients' confidentiality.
